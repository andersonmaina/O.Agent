TOPIC 1: ALGEBRA
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Q1. Solve for x: 3x - 7 = 2x + 5

Step 1: Collect like terms
3x - 2x = 5 + 7

Step 2: Simplify
x = 12

Answer: x = 12

-----------------------------------------------------------------
Q2. Simplify: (2x² + 3x - 5) - (x² - 2x + 3)

Step 1: Remove brackets (distribute negative sign)
= 2x² + 3x - 5 - x² + 2x - 3

Step 2: Collect like terms
= (2x² - x²) + (3x + 2x) + (-5 - 3)
= x² + 5x - 8

Answer: x² + 5x - 8

-----------------------------------------------------------------
Q3. Factorize completely: x² - 9x + 20

Step 1: Find two numbers that multiply to 20 and add to -9
Numbers are -4 and -5

Step 2: Write as factors
= (x - 4)(x - 5)

Answer: (x - 4)(x - 5)

Q4. Solve the simultaneous equations:
    2x + 3y = 13  ...(1)
    3x - 2y = 0   ...(2)

Step 1: From equation (2): 3x = 2y, so x = 2y/3

Step 2: Substitute into equation (1)
2(2y/3) + 3y = 13
4y/3 + 3y = 13
4y/3 + 9y/3 = 13
13y/3 = 13
y = 3

Step 3: Find x
x = 2(3)/3 = 2

Answer: x = 2, y = 3

Q5. If a = 3 and b = -2, evaluate: 2a² - 3ab + b²

Step 1: Substitute values
= 2(3)² - 3(3)(-2) + (-2)²

Step 2: Calculate each term
= 2(9) - 3(-6) + 4
= 18 + 18 + 4
= 40

Answer: 40
